How are points calculated?

Points are calculated by taking the differential for each game, totalled and divided by number of games so Teams which played 3 or 4 or 5 games are treated equally.

For e.g., if the scores are 11/3; 12/10; 9/11; 3/11; 11/4.

Differentials will be 8+2-2-8+7. Total = 7 (for Team A) Points = 7/5 (no of games) = 1.4 for Team A and -1.4 for Team B.

Positive number means the team won. Lower number indicates close games.
